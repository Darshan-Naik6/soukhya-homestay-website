Welcome

Welcome to Soukhya Riverside Homestay

Soukhya Riverside Homestay is a peaceful nature retreat located in the scenic village of Aghanashini near Gokarna, Karnataka.

Situated on the upper floor of a traditional home, the homestay features a large living and dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a private natural environment.

To ensure total privacy and comfort, we only host one group at a time. Enjoy fresh air, natural surroundings, and home-cooked local meals prepared on request.

Whether you are traveling with family, friends, or as a couple, Soukhya provides a calm and refreshing stay experience.
